The Ultimate Destination for Lens Implantation: SU Yonsei!

This surgery serves as a good alternative to laser surgery for those with thin corneas, irregular shapes, or extreme refractive errors that make corneal surgery undesirable. As the procedure neither cuts the cornea nor alters the eye's internal structure, it is the most physiological option available. It also offers excellent nighttime visual acuity. The lens can be inserted either anteriorly or posteriorly depending on the required positioning. It has very low regression rate and you can maintain excellent vision for life.

Astigmatism-Correcting Lenses
Toric lenses are a type of intraocular lens specifically designed to accurately correct astigmatism. This revolutionary surgery involves the implantation of a lens etched with measurements for both myopia and astigmatism, allowing for the correction of high degrees of both in a single operation.
  • Toric Artiflex These options set the standard for rapid recovery and stable astigmatism correction.
  • Toric Artisan They are the go-to choice for cases where combined myopia and astigmatism exceed 15 diopters.
  • EVO+Toric ICL These lenses enable the simultaneous correction of high degrees of both conditions without the need for iridotomy.
  • Bioptics Additionally, LASIK or LASEK can be performed as a secondary operation for further vision correction.
