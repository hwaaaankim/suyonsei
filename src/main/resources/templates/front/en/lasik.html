LASIK

LASIK is a surgical procedure that employs laser technology to create a corneal flap, including the epithelium and a portion of the stroma. The laser is then applied to the stroma to correct vision, after which the corneal flap is repositioned.

  • - Patients generally experience a foreign-body sensation or slight discomfort for 3-4 hours post-operation. However, most can return to their daily activities within a day, making LASIK one of the most convenient surgical options with excellent vision improvement as soon as the day after the procedure.
  • - At SU Yonsei, we utilize a judicious combination of our three top-tier laser platforms as well as Sirius, Vario, and Oculyzer diagnostic tools. Our aim is to exceed your maximum corrected visual acuity with glasses, focusing on achieving superior uncorrected visual outcomes.
