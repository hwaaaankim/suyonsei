Lens Implantation Surgery

Phakic Intraocular Lens implantation

This surgery serves as a good alternative to laser surgery for those with thin corneas, irregular shapes, or extreme refractive errors that make corneal surgery undesirable.

As the procedure neither cuts the cornea nor alters the eye's internal structure, it is the most physiological option available.

It also offers excellent nighttime visual acuity. The lens can be inserted either anteriorly or posteriorly depending on the required positioning. It has very low regression rate and you can maintain excellent vision for life.

LASEK

LASEK

LASEK is a surgical procedure aimed at correcting myopia and astigmatism by removing the outermost epithelium of the cornea and applying laser treatment.

Though the process involves 3-4 days of discomfort and a slower recovery rate, it is considered one of the most conservative laser vision correction surgeries. This procedure is especially recommended for individuals with thin corneas or those who wear thick glasses.

At SU Yonsei Eye Clinic, we believe that the journey to optimal vision extends far beyond the surgical procedure itself. It also involves the meticulous pre-operative evaluation and lifelong post-operative care that our specialized medical team provides. This articulates the conservative yet effective approach taken by LASEK, and how our clinic ensures comprehensive care at each step.

LASIK

LASIK

LASIK is a surgical procedure that employs laser technology to create a corneal flap, including the epithelium and a portion of the stroma.

The laser is then applied to the stroma to correct vision, after which the corneal flap is repositioned. Patients generally experience a foreign-body sensation or slight discomfort for 3-4 hours post-operation. However, most can return to their daily activities within a day, making LASIK one of the most convenient surgical options with excellent vision improvement as soon as the day after the procedure.

At SU Yonsei, we utilize a judicious combination of our three top-tier laser platforms as well as Sirius, Vario, and Oculyzer diagnostic tools. Our aim is to exceed your maximum corrected visual acuity with glasses, focusing on achieving superior uncorrected visual outcomes.

Special Advantages

# Premium Custom Q VisuLASIK

=> Employing Alcon's state-of-the-art EX500 technology, our Custom Q VisuLASIK is an innovative, personalized surgical approach designed to preserve the natural aspheric shape of the cornea.
