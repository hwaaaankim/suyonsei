"Shorter Treatment, Faster Speed"

The laser treatment time with the SMILE PRO is less than 10 seconds. This is achieved through ZEISS's advanced precision scanning technology. The brief laser exposure significantly reduces the risk of suction loss and minimizes the patient's psychological stress.

Scanning Technology

The laser scanning technology of VISUMAX 800 is a pivotal feature that ensures precise laser application to specific areas of the cornea. The accuracy of this scanning technology plays a crucial role in determining the outcome of the surgery and the quality of vision post-procedure. ZEISS's laser scanning technology enables the most sophisticated and precise laser application among all existing lenticule extraction surgeries.

CentraLign

The CentraLign feature of SMILE PRO accurately detects eye movements and automatically aligns with the eye's center, ensuring optimal treatment outcomes.

OcuLign

The OcuLign function offers a more comprehensive solution by conducting precise analysis of the astigmatic axis. It corrects for rotation of the astigmatism axis, providing patients with the best possible treatment outcomes.
