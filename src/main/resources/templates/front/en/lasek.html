LASEK

LASEK is a surgical procedure aimed at correcting myopia and astigmatism by removing the outermost epithelium of the cornea and applying laser treatment.

  • - Though the process involves 3-4 days of discomfort and a slower recovery rate, it is considered one of the most conservative laser vision correction surgeries.
  • - This procedure is especially recommended for individuals with thin corneas or those who wear thick glasses.
  • - At SU Yonsei Eye Clinic, we believe that the journey to optimal vision extends far beyond the surgical procedure itself. It also involves the meticulous pre-operative evaluation and lifelong post-operative care that our specialized medical team provides.
  • - This articulates the conservative yet effective approach taken by LASEK, and how our clinic ensures comprehensive care at each step.

LASEK

  • Premium Custom S-LASEK
  • Premium Custom Q-LASEK
  • Extra LASEK (LASEK with CXL)
  • - Utilizing Schwind's latest Amaris RED with SmartPulse Technology, the Premium Custom S-LASEK procedure is exceptionally precise and sophisticated.
  • - The technology transfers intricate data like Angle Kappa, iris shape, higher-order aberrations, and corneal curvature directly to the Amaris RED laser for meticulous surgery.
  • - Leveraging the specialized expertise of SU Yonsei medical staff, we provide truly personalized surgeries using the Aberration-Free / Corneal Wavefront techniques tailored to individual characteristics.
  • - Employing Alcon's latest EX500 specifications, the Premium Custom Q-LASEK is an innovative custom surgical procedure designed to minimize after-effects such as nighttime glare, myopic regression, and corneal opacities by preserving the natural aspheric shape of the cornea as much as possible.
  • - This procedure is particularly recommended for the correction of extreme levels of myopia and astigmatism.
  • - This text reflects the advanced, customized offerings in LASEK procedures available at SU Yonsei Eye Clinic, emphasizing their precision, sophistication, and personalized approach.
  • - Extra LASEK is a surgical method that combines LASEK refractive correction with Corneal Cross-Linking (CXL) to enhance corneal stability. This is particularly applicable when dealing with thin corneas, extremely high levels of myopia/astigmatism, or hyperopia.
  • - Our medical team at this clinic has been performing corneal cross-linking since 2011, primarily for the treatment of keratoconus. We are the first in the country to have introduced Extra LASEK and have reported its outcomes in academic circles.
  • - Given our extensive knowledge of the pros and cons of this procedure, we strongly recommend consulting with our experts to make an informed decision on whether Extra LASEK is the appropriate treatment for you.
